Introducing Vigor

A Slack bot that keeps your team healthy and productive. Every 2 hours Vigor sends an office exercise to all team members. For free!

100% Safe

Vigor requires minimal permissions. We don't need to read anything postest in your Slack channels.

No Spam

Vigor only sends an exercise when the team member has been online for two hours.

Pause / Restart

Every message from Vigor contains a pause button. Pressing it prevents Vigor from sending more messages.
