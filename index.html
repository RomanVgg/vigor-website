Introducing Vigor

A Slack bot that keeps your team healthy and productive. Every 2 hours Vigor sends an office exercise to all team members. For free!

100% Safe

Vigor requires minimal permissions. We don't need to read anything posted in your Slack channels.

No Spam

Vigor only sends an exercise when the team member has been online and available for two hours.

Pause / Restart

Every message from Vigor contains a pause button. Pressing it stops any further messages from Vigor.
