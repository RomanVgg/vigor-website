Privacy Policy

Introduction

In the following the term "Vigor" is used when referring to the Vigor Slack app offered on http://workvigor.com/. Users of the web service Slack (slack.com) can install this app on their team account. Vigor periodically sends messages to all team members. Vigor is a free, non-commercial service.

Information Vigor collects and receives

Vigor makes sure only information needed to carry out the service is collected and stored. What data Vigor saves:
  1. Slack team account info To be able to send messages to the members of a Slack team, Vigor needs to store the team name, id and API token of every team which signed up.
  2. Slack team member info Vigor also stores name and id of every team member.
  3. Team member presence Vigor only sends messages if the users has been active for a certain time. In order to determine this, Vigor periodically checks the presence of every team member and records when the team member has become active. After the information becomes outdated, Vigor is deleting it. It's not possible to create a long-term activity profile with this data.
  4. Log data Vigor automatically collects information on the usage of the application (messages sent out, users pausing the application etc.)
Vigor does NOT receive or save:
  • any messages users post in channels or via direct messages
  • user email addresses
  • team or user info beyond the mentioned items

Vigor's  Cookie Policy & Website Tracking

Vigor uses third party services like Google Analytics for website analytics. You may opt-out of third party cookies from Google Analytics on its website. We do not currently recognize or respond to browser-initiated Do Not Track signals as there is no consistent industry standard for compliance.
